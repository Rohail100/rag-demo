# FAQ

## Account
You can create an account using your email address or sign in with Google or Apple. To reset your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Account settings allow you to update your profile, saved addresses, and payment methods.

## Orders
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement by visiting your order history. Once an order enters processing, changes are no longer possible. For assistance with orders past the 1-hour window, contact support.

## Payments
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely. Your payment information is never stored on our servers. Sales tax is calculated based on your shipping address.

## Customer Support
Support hours are Monday through Friday, 9AM to 5PM EST. You can reach us by email at support@example.com or through the live chat widget on our website. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days.
