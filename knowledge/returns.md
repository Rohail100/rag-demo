# Returns

## Standard Return Policy
Customers may return products within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original packaging with receipt or order number. Products must be unused and in original condition.

## Exceptions
Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned. Electronics must be returned within 14 days. Personalized or custom items cannot be returned unless defective.

## Refund Process
Refunds are processed within 7 business days after the returned item is received. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

## International Returns
International returns have a 21-day return window. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Customs fees and duties are non-refundable.
