# Shipping

## Domestic Shipping
Standard domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for next-day delivery. We ship to all 50 US states and military APO addresses.

## International Shipping
International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on destination. Customs processing may cause additional delays. We ship to over 60 countries worldwide.

## Shipping Costs
Free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard shipping is $5.99 for orders under $50. Express shipping starts at $12.99. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

## Order Tracking
You will receive a confirmation email with your order details immediately after purchase. A tracking number is emailed once your order ships. Tracking updates are available via our website or the carrier's portal.
